The Problem With Writing About Soccer

It seems to me that there's a great deal of incredibly sub-par American soccer writing these days. It should really come as no surprise though, as many of us Americans see through the corrupt farce that is FIFA simply cannot appreciate the nuances of The Beautiful Game. The internet has enabled an even further decline of modern soccer writing: Looking at certain articles from Soccer Ipsum, we can see that some writers cannot even string together a coherent sentence: Four-four-two goalie one-two center-half striker midfielder UEFA European Championship World Cup yellow card chip number 10 goal ball referee forward African Cup of Nations.
